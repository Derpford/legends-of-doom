The ammo and crate sprites are by dawnbreez (that's me!). So are SPRKA0 and SPRKB0.

The sprites for Strawberry Jam and Spicy Water are from Ac!d: https://forum.zdoom.org/viewtopic.php?t=69513

Bloodsaw sprites are from Craneo: https://forum.zdoom.org/viewtopic.php?t=65146

Surplus Caliber's sprite, Z-Sec Goggles' sprite, and the small/large health packs are from BloodyAcid's mishmash of edits: https://forum.zdoom.org/viewtopic.php?t=33595
So are the sprites for the Power Amp, Regen Pack, MOLLE Plate, Aim Comp, and Leaky Backpack.

The Copied, Treasured, and Corrupted Floppy sprites were made by TommyGalano5.
